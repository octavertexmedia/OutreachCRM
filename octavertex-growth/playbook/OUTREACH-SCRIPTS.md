# Outreach Scripts — LinkedIn, Email, Follow-ups

Tone: peer-to-peer operator language. No “synergy.” No “cutting-edge.” Specific > clever.

Replace brackets. Always personalize first line with a real observation (website, product, city, trade show).

---

## 1. LinkedIn connection note (≤300 chars)

**A — Packaging / manufacturing**

> Hi {{Name}} — noticed {{Company}} supplies {{product/category}} out of {{city}}. We build catalogue + quote websites for manufacturers (recently a Mumbai packaging firm). Open to connecting?

**B — Exporters**

> Hi {{Name}} — saw {{Company}} exports {{category}}. Many exporters still run on PDFs + WhatsApp only. We build SEO-ready catalogue platforms for B2B. Happy to connect.

**C — Soft (if note gets ignored often, connect with no note, then message)**

> Thanks for connecting, {{Name}}. Curious—are inbound enquiries mostly WhatsApp/referral today, or is the website actually contributing?

## 6. Discovery call agenda (45 min)

1. **5 min** — Their business & sales motion  
2. **10 min** — Where leads come from / leak  
3. **10 min** — Current website / tools pain  
4. **10 min** — Show 1 case (Intellect Kraft) + package map  
5. **5 min** — Fit check: budget band, timeline, decision process  
6. **5 min** — Next step: proposal date or polite no  

**Must ask**

- “What does success look like in 90 days?”  
- “Who else is involved in approving ₹1L+?”  
- “Have you budgeted, or is this exploratory?”  

If exploratory with no budget → nurture, don’t write a free custom proposal.

## 8. Phrases to delete from your vocabulary

| Avoid | Use |
|-------|-----|
| Full-stack developer | Digital systems partner for B2B |
| It’ll be quick / few days | Fixed-scope platform, 4–6 week delivery |
| Cheap / affordable | Clear investment bands |
| I’ll do whatever you want | Here’s the package that matches your goal |
