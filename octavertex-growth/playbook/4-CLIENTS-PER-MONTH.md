# Operating System: ≥4 High-Paying Clients / Month

Aligned to *Premium Web Development Business Strategy*.

---

## 1. The math (so the goal is operational, not aspirational)

Assume average **won deal = ₹1.5L** (Lead Generation Platform band).

| Funnel stage | Monthly target | Notes |
|--------------|----------------|-------|
| Qualified discovery calls | **20** | Decision-maker + ₹1L+ budget signal |
| Written proposals | **12** | ~60% of calls |
| Wins | **4** | ~33% close on proposals (stretch; improve with case studies) |
| Revenue (projects) | **₹6L** | Before retainers |

### Upstream volume (outbound)

Daily (5 days/week):

| Activity | /day | /month (~22 days) |
|----------|------|-------------------|
| LinkedIn connection requests (ICP) | 20 | ~440 |
| Personalized emails / InMails | 20 | ~440 |
| Follow-ups | 10 | ~220 |

**Rule:** If discoveries < 5/week for two weeks, increase outbound—don’t discount price.

---

## 2. ICP (ideal client profile)

**Firmographic**

- Industry: manufacturing, packaging, export, hospitality, healthcare, logistics, B2B services  
- Geography: India (Mumbai / Pune / Gujarat / TN / North manufacturing belts first)  
- Size: roughly ₹2Cr–₹100Cr revenue or clear scaling stage  
- Trigger: new product line, export push, bad website, lost tenders, competitor ranking on Google  

**Buyer titles**

- Owner / Partner  
- Sales Director  
- Marketing Manager (with owner access)  
- Operations head (catalogue-heavy businesses)  

**Disqualify fast**

- “Need something under ₹40k”  
- No decision-maker on call  
- “Just redesign colours” with no lead goal  

---

## 3. Offer ladder (what you sell every time)

1. **Business Website** — ₹75k–₹1.25L  
2. **Lead Generation Platform** — ₹1.25L–₹2L ← *default recommendation*  
3. **Business Automation Platform** — ₹2L–₹4L+  

Always recommend **#2** on discovery unless they truly only need trust pages.

**Never lead with hours.** Lead with outcomes (catalogue → quote → CRM → SEO).

---

## 4. Weekly rhythm

### Monday — Pipeline & targets

- Review CRM: stages, ages, next actions  
- Set week targets: 5 discoveries · 3 proposals · 1 win minimum  

### Tue–Thu — Outbound blocks (2× 90 min)

- Morning: LinkedIn + research  
- Afternoon: email + follow-ups  
- No “random scrolling”—only ICP lists  

### Friday — Authority + proposals

- Publish 1 asset: case study snippet / before-after / SEO note / AI automation example  
- Close open proposals; send Friday follow-ups  

### Daily non-negotiables

- 20 LI · 20 emails · 10 follow-ups  
- Log every touch in CRM  

---

## 5. Pipeline stages (CRM)

```
Lead → Connected → Discovery booked → Discovery done → Proposal sent → Negotiation → Won / Lost → Retainer
```

**SLA**

- Reply to inbound < 4 business hours  
- Proposal < 3 business days after discovery  
- Follow-up cadence: Day 2 · Day 5 · Day 10 · Day 21 (then nurture)

## 7. Pricing progression (discipline)

| Stage | Floor price |
|-------|-------------|
| Now (positioning reset) | **₹1.25L** for Lead Platform |
| After 2 strong case studies | ₹1.5L |
| After retainer base | ₹2L+ default |

Say no to ₹25k “full websites.” Offer a **phase 1** only if it still clears ₹75k and leads to Platform.

---

## 8. Capacity for 4 wins / month

Rough delivery load at 4× Lead Platforms:

- ~4–6 concurrent builds if timelines overlap → need **you + 1 contractor** (frontend/content) by month 2–3  
- Protect discovery calendar: max 2 sales calls/day  

If delivery slips, **pause outbound for 3 days**—reputation > volume.

## 9. Scoreboard (track weekly)

| KPI | Green | Yellow | Red |
|-----|-------|--------|-----|
| Discoveries | ≥5 | 3–4 | <3 |
| Proposals out | ≥3 | 2 | ≤1 |
| Wins | ≥1 | 0 but late-stage | 0 and empty pipeline |
| Outbound days hit | 5/5 | 3–4 | ≤2 |
| Content published | 1 | 0 | 0 for 2 weeks |

Month win = **4 closed** or **₹5L+ signed** (whichever you choose as north star—prefer count + revenue).
