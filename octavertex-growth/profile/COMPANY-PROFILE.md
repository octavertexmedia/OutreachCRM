## 4. Proof of work (anchor case)

### Intellect Kraft — Packaging manufacturer / B2B supply (Mumbai)

**Problem:** Needed a credible digital presence and a sales-ready catalogue—not a static brochure—for Maharashtra food-service buyers.

**Solution:** Custom Wagtail platform with product catalogue, QR-enabled mobile e-catalogue, quote cart, CRM, technical SEO, location/research content, and Contabo VPS production deployment.

**Outcome for the buyer:**

- One system for brand, catalogue, and lead capture  
- Sales can share a WhatsApp-ready catalogue URL  
- SEO foundation for “paper cups Mumbai / packaging Maharashtra” demand  
- Admin ownership without locking into a black-box agency theme  

**How we talk about it (never by hours):**  
> A scalable business platform: custom CMS, CRM, SEO-ready architecture, QR-enabled catalogue, production deployment, and an admin experience that lowers long-term maintenance cost.

Market value for this class of build: typically **₹2.5L–₹5L+** at agency rates; we productize clear scopes starting **₹1.25L+** so mid-market manufacturers can buy without enterprise bloat.
