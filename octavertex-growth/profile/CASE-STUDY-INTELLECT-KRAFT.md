# Case Study — Intellect Kraft  
### Custom B2B packaging platform (Mumbai)

**Industry:** Biodegradable food-service packaging · Manufacturing / wholesale  
**Engagement type:** Lead Generation / Business Automation Platform  
**Stack (for technical readers):** Django + Wagtail CMS · product catalogue · mobile QR e-catalogue · quote cart · CRM · technical SEO · Contabo VPS  

---

## Problem

Intellect Kraft needed a digital system that matched how packaging buyers actually purchase: browse categories, share a catalogue link, request quotes on WhatsApp—and appear for local search demand across Maharashtra. A static brochure site would not support sales or SEO.

## Solution

Octavertex designed and shipped a production platform including:

- Custom CMS for pages, products, and content  
- Full product catalogue with category structure  
- QR-friendly mobile e-catalogue for field / WhatsApp sharing  
- Quote cart and enquiry capture into CRM  
- Technical SEO (structure, meta, schema foundations, location/research content)  
- Hardened production deployment on VPS with SSL  

## Outcome

- Single owned system for **brand + catalogue + leads**  
- Sales-ready mobile catalogue URL for buyer conversations  
- SEO architecture aimed at packaging / city demand—not plugin theatre  
- Admin team can update products and pages without developer dependency for routine content  

## How we describe the value (client-facing)

> A scalable business platform with a custom CMS, CRM, SEO-ready architecture, QR-enabled catalogue, production deployment, and an admin experience that reduces long-term maintenance cost.

**Not:** “We built a website in five days.”

## Investment band (market context)

Comparable custom agency builds for this scope often sit at **₹2.5L–₹5L+**.  
Octavertex productizes similar outcomes for mid-market manufacturers typically from **₹1.25L–₹2.5L** depending on catalogue size and CRM depth.
